As I know that i have a CONCRETE SEQUENTIAL style of thinking ,i keep asking myself how to use this to maximise my learning at EDA.Because i believe although its not impossible to change the style its quite unnatural to happen. So instead of changing the style of learning i would rathe find a way to work aroun it so that i can achieve "MY BEST" After some research on that i found its quite possible and every style has its advantages and disadvantages.

Concrete Sequential Learners work best when they
  • have an orderly quiet environment.
  • know the accepted way of doing things.
  • have exact directions and examples.
  • can be consistent and efficient.
  • face limited change in predictable situation.
  • are given approval for specific work done.
  • can apply ideas in a practical hands-on way.
  • can answer, "How does it work?"
  • are able to trust that others will follow through.
  • have guided practice with an organized person.
Knowing the learning style
  • increases your self-confidence
  • improves your self-image
  • teaches you how to use your brain best
  • gives you insight into your strengths, weaknesses and habits
  • enables you to enjoy any learning process
  • inspires greater curiosity, motivation for lifelong learning
  • shows you how to take advantage of your natural skills & inclinations

As far as the mindsets go, i learned in the second week into the phase 0 at EDA that i have to care less about what people think of me and concentrate on how i can learn the maximum.I am not afraid of making mistakes or asking for help about anything,but sure want to be the best what i can be.After that i am noticing a change in my grasping.i might be slow compared to others ,but thats no camparision.i Just have to keep studying untill i get it.That boosts my confidence.I think i am definitely thinking in a broader way than i used to.And hoping to get better 1 inch everyday.
